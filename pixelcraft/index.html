06 / FAQ

Good questions, direct answers

Before you
hit register.

Who can participate in VertechX?+

Events are designed for college students across disciplines. Final eligibility, dates and college-ID requirements are confirmed during official registration.

What should I bring?+

Carry a valid college ID and any equipment listed in your event details. Participants should also bring a laptop and charger where required.

Can I register for more than one event?+

Yes. Select multiple events during registration and review the complete list before confirming your entry.

How does team registration work?+

Choose the event first and register with the participant details shown in the form. Team-size requirements are listed on every event card.
